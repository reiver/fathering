Showering

eagerness

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux on Monday June 29th, 2026

I found that if I let my kids take some of their toys to the shower, and after the showering is done let them play with their toys in the shower for a while, that getting them to go to the shower is easier.

They seem to tend to stop protesting going to the shower this way. And, even seem to look forward to it.
