Yesterday I started doing something more deliberate to continue to help my (5 year old) son learn to read.

I don't want to just rely on the schools he is going to. I want to help accelerate his learning.

One thing I did yesterday was that I purchased Bob Books set 1 through set 5. I don't now much about them, but — I saw a recommendation for them as phonics based and science of reading (SoR) based tools for learning to read.

They were delivered.
